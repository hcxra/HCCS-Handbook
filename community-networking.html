Engage with Clubs

At Hunter College, there are a few ways to engage with your fellow Computer Science students outside of class. One way is through clubs, specifically the Computer Science Club and the Women in Computer Science Club. Both clubs have frequent meetups, ranging from meet-and-greets, hackathons, interview practice, group meals, trips to companies, and holiday celebrations.

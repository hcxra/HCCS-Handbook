About Us

This Handbook was created by 4 undergraduate Computer Science students enrolled at Hunter College. We wanted to help incoming and current computer science majors plan their course route, get networking advice, learn which tools to use, and more.

With the knowledge and tools we wish we had when entering the field of computer science, we hope to assist students in getting ready for their future careers in technology.
